Product & UX Research Consulting

Coverd — Case Study

Product consultant focused on user research and positioning for a Gen Z finance startup.

  • Role: Product & UX Research Consultant (Ascend Consulting Group)
  • Context: Early-stage fintech building savings and credit tools for Gen Z
  • Impact: Reframed positioning and roadmap priorities based on 50+ user interviews

Background

Coverd was testing a savings and credit product for Gen Z. Founders needed to understand user motivations, competitive gaps, and the language that resonated to position the product and prioritize features.

My Role

  • Designed interview frameworks to probe spending habits, savings triggers, and credit comfort
  • Led 50+ user interviews across students and early-career professionals
  • Synthesized findings weekly into themes, personas, and “jobs to be done”
  • Delivered positioning and roadmap recommendations directly to founders

Research Approach

Built a research plan with screeners, interview guides, and consent scripts; executed virtual interviews; coded transcripts to surface themes; and pressure-tested insights with the founding team before recommending product and messaging changes.

Impact

Shifted positioning toward “confidence with cash flow” versus “credit building,” informed MVP sequencing to prioritize savings nudges over advanced credit features, and provided messaging that raised opt-in intent in follow-up tests.

Artifacts

Redacted research frameworks, interview guide excerpts, and slide snapshots summarizing key themes (available upon request).

Reflection

Reinforced the value of early, qualitative signal in PMF discovery, nuances of Gen Z trust in finance products, and the need to translate research into crisp product storytelling.

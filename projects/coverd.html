Ascend Consulting Group and Coverd

In this 8-week tech product consulting engagement, I worked on a team of 8 student consultants focusing on delivering Gen-Z tailored UX insights for Coverd: an early stage, VC-backed gamified fintech startup.

What is Coverd?

Coverd is an early stage, VC-backed fintech startup that focuses on making personal finance fun for users. The app lets users play games, win prizes, and track spending in a fun, casino-style environment.

What is Ascend Consulting Group?

Ascend Consulting Group is a network of student consultancies at universities across the nation that specializes in delivering Gen-Z perspectives and feedback to the tech startups of tomorrow. ACG provides tech product consulting services, focusing on software and startups.

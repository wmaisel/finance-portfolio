Santa Barbara Gift Baskets — Case Study

Engagement Manager leading a full acquisition-prep and operations upgrade for a local retailer.

  • Role: EM, 5-person student consulting team
  • Scope: Acquisition readiness, operations, web presence
  • Deliverables: SOP binder, SEO fixes, buyer pitch deck

Client Background

Santa Barbara Gift Baskets is a local retailer preparing for acquisition. The owner needed better process documentation, a cleaner web presence, and a narrative for prospective buyers.

My Role

  • Led a 5-person team through weekly sprints and client check-ins
  • Scoped and sequenced deliverables tied to acquisition milestones
  • Delegated workstreams, reviewed outputs, and ensured client-ready quality

Workstreams

SEO / SMO Improvements

Audited site performance and metadata; implemented image compression, alt text, and meta description fixes; established a review mechanism to keep listings current.

Operational SOP Creation

Documented core processes (fulfillment, vendor management, customer service) into an SOP binder with checklists and versioning guidance.

Acquisition Pitch Development

Built a buyer narrative highlighting revenue streams, operational readiness, and near-term upside; created a deck with key metrics and process visuals.

Impact

Delivered an acquisition-ready SOP manual, improved site presentation and discoverability, and equipped the owner with a concise buyer pitch to support negotiations.

Artifacts

Redacted SOP pages, SEO audit notes, and pitch deck snapshots available on request.

Reflection

Learned to balance near-term operational fixes with acquisition narrative needs, and refined leadership cadence for a multi-workstream student team.
